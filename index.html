    An ethereal pilgrimage venture in Tirupati, with a modern twist

    Experience our colossal, luxury homes and Villas near Rural Tirupati with top-notch facilities and a serene natural ambiance that make a dreamy destination for Pilgrimage stays, weddings, Resorts, or as a residence.

    Fully- Equipped with top amenities

    Prasanthi Nagar in Tirupati is spread over 12 acres, equipped with luxury and extensive amenities like Sports center, Ayurveda vaidya shala, a children’s park, shuttle court, a swimming pool, celebration spaces, etc. and can be a dreamy destination with a perfect balance of tradition and modernity.

    TUDA Approved layout

    The layout is approved by TUDA and is built after careful consideration of Vaastu by experts. The space is well planned, has a continuous supply of high-quality drinking water, professionally designed drainage water systems, systematic network of B.T Roads, and a treatment plant for purified water supply.

    Heart to Top destinations in Tirupathi

    Prasanthi nagar is located at an accessible spot adjoining Rural Tirupati, and in close proximity to Railway station, Airport, Colleges, Hospital and connects major roadways in and out of Tirupati. It has a distance of 100 m from the proposed 150 ft masterplan road and 1km from the outer ring-road.

    For Stay, Rent and Residence

    Suite rooms, 1 bhk, 2 bhk and independent houses, there are a wide range and types of flats for rent, lease, sale or short-term pilgrimage stays that assures you an euphoric experience, never forgotten.

    Carefully-considered Vaastu

    Vaastu is an integral part of our venture and we’ve laid our foundation after meticulously studying the vaastu of each and every structure built. Prasanthi nagar is east-faced surrounded by hills to its North and South. A pond is located at the north and there’s a water channel that flows water from the South East to North East. Overall, the site at Prasanthi Nagar is accurately built with the right Vaastu to serve as a good Omen and to contribute to the sentiments, peace of mind and happy living.

    Picturesque appearance

    The first glance of Prasanthi nagar and the surroundings give you a feel as if it’s alive straight out from a magazine. From the vast expanse of greenery, to the gleaming waters reflecting the sunlight, to the dainty mountains surrounding the scenery and not forgetting the divine Tirumala mountains that stand proud blessing the land surrounding it, the scenery is a magical sight to behold. Anyone residing Prasanthi nagar is assured to experience the bliss of nature that leaves that wanting for more.
