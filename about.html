About - PRASANTHINAGAR

An Exquisite Experience for Spiritual and Nature Lovers

Prasanthi Nagar is a dream venture built after years of thought process put forward to create the finest staying experience at Tirupathi. With mesmerizing surroundings, thoroughly planned Vaastu and an extensive range of high-quality, modern amenities- Prasanthi nagar is a place built with a classic blend of luxury, modernity and tradition, aimed for spiritual growth as well as relaxation. Prasanthi Nagar can be anything of a residence, wedding destination, celebration space, resort or a spiritual venture that rekindles fulfillment in every way possible.
The team behind Prasanthinagar Mr. Venkateshwarao Chemalamudi, Mr. Moorthy Chirukuri and team, has executed a good number of successful projects in a decade, and are widely recognized for their high-quality work and unique thought process. The team holds approximately 25 years of Marketing and real-estate experience and is keen to put all the knowledge garnered into Prasanthinagar, their dream venture. This dream venture holds a lot of benevolence for the team as it’s a part and parcel of their passion, apart from income. The team collectively believes in the motto of creating excellence and enriching experiences and is striving hard to achieve the same.
